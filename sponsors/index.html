Service Sponsorship

No cash? Sponsor with your services.

Beauty, wellness, and creative businesses — you can be part of this journey by sponsoring in trade. I'll represent your brand just as proudly.

How it works

Provide services that support my competition prep. In return, you receive social media exposure and jacket recognition calibrated to the estimated retail value of what you offer.

Estimated value → tier equivalent:

  • $1,000+ in services → Heart of Texas (Premier) benefits
  • $500–$999 in services → Lone Star (Gold) benefits
  • $250–$499 in services → Bluebonnet (Silver) benefits
  • Up to $249 in services → Wildflower (Bronze) benefits

Service sponsors receive the same jacket placement and benefits as their matched cash tier.

My Platform

The cause behind the crown

Every title needs a purpose. Mine is the Little Visionaries Foundation — my own initiative providing glasses to kids who need them but can't always get them.

Little Visionaries Foundation

So many kids fall behind in school simply because they can't see the board — and their families can't always cover the cost of an exam or a pair of glasses. The Little Visionaries Foundation exists to close that gap, one child at a time. It's the platform I carry with me into every pageant appearance, and the story I tell on stage at America's Most Beautiful Miss.

1 in 4 school-age kids needs glasses to see the board, read, or learn
$50 covers a full eye exam + glasses for one child
95% of kids in underserved communities who need glasses don't have them

Sponsors at every tier help fund this mission directly — and the Little Visionaries Foundation is featured across all my pageant content.
